# Haywire - Class Skills

## Tier 1 Classes

### Advisor
- **LOCAL SQUAD:** This unit is always deployed with 3 Local Fighters.
- **MENTOR:** Local fighters within 3" of the advisor may reroll 1 failed combat action per turn.

### Assault
- **GRENADE BELT:** This model may carry any number of grenades but can only use one per turn.
- **40 MIKE MIKE:** When using a grenade launcher, cover is ignored when doing the combat roll.

### Breacher
- **DOORKICKER:** This unit is equipped with breaching charges. They can be used to open any door or create a 1" hole in any wall and each costs 1AP to use. Models located on the other side of the point of breach and within 3" are pinned.
- **REMOTE SENSING:** If this unit is in base contact with a door, he may reveal what's inside following the "threat inside building" rule (1-14 = nobody, 15-17 = 1 civilian, 18-19 = 1 OPFOR, 20 = 2 OPFOR).

### Combat Lifesaver
- **FIELD SURGEON:** May heal 1 point of damage on any injured model. This action cost 2AP.

### Demolition
- **SHOCKWAVE:** When using explosive devices, models within 6" of the point of impact and in LOS of that point are suppressed. They will start their next activation with a single AP.
- **DEMO CHARGE:** This unit is equipped with a demolition charge. He can use 1AP to place the charge and use another AP detonate it using a remote trigger. Each action use the interact rule. The charge has a 3" blast radius and a 12 penetration. Anything within the blast radius is killed. No roll is needed to detonate the charge.

### Deniable Asset
- **CIVILIAN CLOTHING:** This model wears civilian type clothing and blends in better in the civilian population. Detection roll needs a 16+.
- **DOUBLE AGENT:** Once per game, the designated OPFOR model (other than the enemy leader) joins your force. He can be located anywhere on the board and will remains undetected as long as he doesn't fire on any enemy target. This action cost 1AP.

### Dog Handler
- **FUR MISSILE:** This model comes with a dog that stays in base contact with him. The dog can be sent to attack an OPFOR model up to 12". The dog can't kill any models but can inflict suppression points. On a 6+ the OPFOR model is suppressed, on a 10+ he is pinned. The dog comes back if the roll is below 6 or if the target is killed. OPFOR models may target the dog using a hand combat stat. This action cost 1AP.
- **FRIENDS FOR LIFE:** If the dog handler is hit by an OPFOR combat action and if the dog is in base contact with him, the dog is killed instead.

### Drone Specialist
- **RECON DRONE:** Deploys a small recon drone on the board in base contact with this unit. AP can then be used to move the drone up to 10" per move action. The drone can reveal threat token in the same way as any models but is harder to detect. It can only be detected on a 17+. Deploying the drone cost 1AP and it can remain in flight for any number of turns. When not in use, the drone hovers in place.

### Gunner
- **TRAVERSING FIRE:** When firing a machine gun. This unit may spread the 2 combat dice on 2 different targets separated from up to 3". This action cost 1AP.
- **BEATEN ZONE:** Fire at all visible OPFOR models. All enemy models in LOS receive 2 suppression points. This action cost 2AP.

### Negotiator
- **CONVINCING:** The target OPFOR model in LOS of the negotiator and within 12" can't fire this turn. This action cost 1AP.
- **GUNSLINGER:** This unit may use a third action point to do a combat action using a pistol.

### Pointman
- **CQB SPECIALIST:** Once per turn, if this model kills an OPFOR model within 6", he is granted a free combat action against another OPFOR model located up to 3" from the first one.
- **COLD BLOODED:** The pointman is not affected by suppression.

### Recon
- **STEADY HANDS:** If this model didn't move this turn he may reroll 1 dice on any combat action.
- **HIGH ALERT:** When a fog of card is drawn, discard it without reading its effect. This action can only be used once per game.

### Saboteur
- **SABOTAGE:** The saboteur has sabotaged a vehicle before going into the mission. The target enemy vehicle is disabled and all OPFOR models inside disembark. This skill can be used on any vehicle including new ones that arrive on a spawn point. This skill doesn't cost any AP but can only be used once per game.
- **DEMO CHARGE:** This unit is equipped with a demolition charge. He can use 1AP to place the charge and detonate it. The charge has a 3" blast radius and a 12 penetration. Anything within the blast radius is killed. No roll is needed to detonate the charge.

### Shield Operator
- **PHALANX:** This model carry a shield protecting him from fire in a 90° angle in front of him. Only a hard shot can hit this model from its front. This unit counts as cover for all other unit behind him.
- **STROBES:** The shield is equipped with powerful flashlights that can blind close enemy. OPFOR models located within 6" and within a 90° angle of this unit are suppressed.

### Sniper
- **DEATH FROM ABOVE:** Killing an enemy at a lower elevation allows you to take another shot at another model in LOS within 6" of the first one. That 2nd shot use the Hard combat stat.
- **CLAYMORE MINE:** This model may deploy a directional landmine that has a 90° cone angle and that can be remote detonated at any moment. Any models within the cone angle and within 6" are hit on a 6+. Placing the mine cost 1AP. The mine can then be detonated using 1AP in the player phase or can also be detonated in the OPFOR phase if this unit is placed in overwatch.

### Spotter
- **AIM ASSIST:** When this model is within 3" of a sniper, the sniper receives another AP at the start of its activation.
- **GUARDIAN:** This unit may fire twice while in overwatch.

### Support
- **SUPPRESSIVE FIRE:** Target a point on the board in LOS of this model. Models within 1,5" of this point can't be activated this turn. This action cost 1AP and ends this model turn.

### Team Leader
- **EMPOWER:** Give 1 of his AP to another friendly model on the board.
- **SUPPORT:** The team leader may call in a support card. This action cost 2AP.
- **THREAT ASSESSMENT:** Overwatch can be activated even after using 2AP to do 2 move, sneak or stalk actions.

### Undercover Agent
- **UNDERCOVER:** The undercover agent starts the game with its weapon hidden and in civilian clothing undetected. He can only be detected when firing at an enemy model in LOS of another enemy or if an enemy is within 6" of him and in LOS.
- **LOCKPICK:** This unit may open any locked door without making any noise. This action cost 2AP and no roll is needed.

## Tier 2 Classes

### AT Specialist
- **SHOCKWAVE:** When using explosive devices, models within 6" of the point of impact and in LOS of that point are suppressed. They will start their next activation with a single AP.

### Automatic Rifleman
- **SUPPRESSIVE FIRE:** Target a point on the board in LOS of the Automatic rifleman. Models within 1,5" of this point can't be activated this turn. This action cost 1AP and ends this model turn.

### Fire Team Leader
- **EMPOWER:** Give 1 of his AP to another friendly model on the board.
- **THREAT ASSESSMENT:** Overwatch can be activated even after using 2AP to do a move or sneak action.

### Grenadier
- **40 MIKE MIKE:** When using a grenade launcher, cover is ignored when doing the combat roll.

### Machine Gunner
- **TRAVERSING FIRE:** When firing a machine gun. This unit may spread the 2 combat dice on 2 different targets separated from up to 3". This action cost 1AP.
- **BEATEN ZONE:** Fire at all visible OPFOR models. All enemy models in LOS receive 2 suppression points. This action cost 2AP.

### Marksman
- **STEADY HANDS:** If this model didn't move this turn he may reroll 1 dice on a combat action. This skill can only be used once per turn.

### Medic
- **FIELD SURGEON:** May heal 1 point of damage on any injured model. This action cost 2AP.

### Rifleman
- **GRENADE BELT:** This unit may carry any number of grenades but can only use one per turn.

### Squad Leader
- **EMPOWER:** Give 1 of his AP to another friendly model on the board.
- **SUPPORT:** The team leader may call in a support card. This action cost 2AP.

---

## Tier 3 Classes

### Interpreter
- **DIALECT:** When within 1" of a civilian model, reveal 1 placement token.

### Local Fighter
- **ARMORED TRANSPORT:** When a local fighter is deployed in a mission, the whole force may be deployed using technicals equipped with turrets on the back or up armored humvees.
